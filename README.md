# 📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard in Power BI

---

# 🔎 Project Summary

This project is a comprehensive **Sales Analysis Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights. The solution provides an interactive reporting experience through multiple dashboard pages, allowing users to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product performance, and employee contributions.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

The dashboard enables users to:

- 📈 Monitor key sales performance indicators.
- 💰 Analyze revenue and sales trends.
- 👥 Evaluate customer purchasing behavior.
- 📦 Analyze product category performance.
- 👨‍💼 Measure employee sales performance.
- 🌍 Compare sales across countries and regions.
- 🎯 Support business decisions through interactive analytics.

---

# 🏢 Dashboard Pages

## 📌 Overview

Provides a high-level summary of business performance including:

- Total Orders
- Total Revenue
- Total Quantity
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Total Sales
- Revenue by Month
- Revenue by Country
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue Trend by Year

---

## 👥 Customers

Customer-focused analytics including:

- Revenue by Country
- Top Customers by Revenue
- Quantity by Company
- Customer Sales Distribution

---

## 📦 Products

Product performance analysis including:

- Revenue by Category
- Quantity by Category
- Revenue vs Quantity Analysis
- Product Performance Table
- Discount Analysis

---

## 👨‍💼 Employees

Employee performance insights including:

- Orders by City
- Orders by Employee
- Employee Sales Contribution
- Sales Summary Table
